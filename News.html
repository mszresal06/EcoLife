ABOUT MALAYSIA ANIMALS

Stay updated on Malaysia's wildlife and conservation efforts to protect endangered species.

FEATURED NEWS

Malayan Tapir

The Malayan tapir is the only Asian tapir species. Unfortunately, fewer than 2,500 are left in the wild due to habitat loss.

Malayan Tiger

Once thriving in Malaysia, fewer than 150 Malayan tigers remain today, facing extinction due to poaching and habitat destruction.

Bornean Orangutan

Bornean orangutan populations have declined over 50% in 60 years. Conservation efforts are critical for their survival.
