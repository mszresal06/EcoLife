UPCOMING PROJECTS

At EcoLife, we are committed to protecting wildlife and preserving the environment. Through collaborative efforts with communities around the world, we tackle issues affecting wildlife and their habitats to create a sustainable future for all.

Below are some of our upcoming projects. Join us in making a tangible impact—whether you're passionate about protecting elephants, rescuing animals in need, or cleaning up oceans to save marine life. Together, we can create lasting change.

Work with Elephants

Southeast Asia

This project focuses on rescuing and rehabilitating elephants in Southeast Asia. Volunteers will assist in feeding, bathing, and monitoring rescued elephants, as well as help restore their natural habitats. Your work will support local communities in coexisting peacefully with these majestic creatures.

Local Animal Rescue

Central America

Our team in Central America works tirelessly to rescue injured and abandoned animals. Volunteers will participate in rehabilitation efforts, help operate shelters, and educate local communities about the importance of animal welfare. Be part of the movement to give these animals a second chance at life.

Ocean Cleanups

North America

Join EcoLife in North America as we tackle the growing problem of ocean pollution. Volunteers will work on beach cleanups, support marine habitat restoration projects, and spread awareness about reducing plastic waste. Together, we can protect marine life and ensure healthier oceans for future generations.
